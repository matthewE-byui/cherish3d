Product Description

Each figurine is crafted from a detailed 3D scan, then 3D printed using FDM technology. The material is UV- and heat-sensitive, so figurines should be displayed indoors and away from direct sunlight or high heat to prevent warping or fading. Available in plain or multi-color options, with sizes ranging from 3–5 inches. Custom sizing is available upon request.
