About Cherish3D

What service are we providing?

We provide 3D printing of memorable keepsakes by 3D scanning real people, babies, and athletes — creating tangible, high-quality miniature sculptures you can hold.

The problem we solve

People can't physically hold their past selves or loved ones. Photos capture moments, but we make those moments tangible again — a mother can hold her baby's tiny hand, a bride can hold a keepsake from her wedding day, and sports fans can remember their champion moments.

Our exact solution

We 3D scan the subject in the moment, create a high-fidelity model, and 3D print and finish a keepsake that represents the real person — not a caricature. The result is a physical, lasting memory you can hold.

What makes us different

Unlike businesses that turn photos into novelty bobbleheads, we 3D scan the real person and reproduce them. Our keepsakes are authentic, detailed, and designed to be cherished — and priced at a fraction of the cost of custom figurine services.
